Our story

Built by a manager.
For managers.

Fuzion Pilot was born from a simple frustration: no tool on the market truly met the needs of agency managers. So we built it ourselves.

We lived through the same struggles as you

Before creating Fuzion Pilot, we were running our own agencies. We know the reality on the ground: Excel spreadsheets to track chatters, WhatsApp messages lost for content requests, 15 tabs open to monitor performance.

"I didn't build a tool for the market. I built the tool I dreamed of having when I was running my own agency."
EK
Ewen K.
Founder, LCX Agency

Every feature in Fuzion Pilot was designed, tested, and iterated by active managers. Not by developers disconnected from the field. That's why the tool is so precise and adapted — because it comes from people who live your daily reality.

Features

Everything to manage your agency at 100%

An all-powerful tool built by agency managers who know exactly what you need.

Real-time Dashboard

360-degree view of your agency: followers, team, revenue, urgent tasks and KPIs — all at a glance.

Chatter Management

Real-time tracking: revenue, PPV, tips per chatter and per model. Identify your top performers.

Complete Outreach

Full pipeline from A to Z: leads, DMs, response rates, booked calls, signatures — all trackable.

Scripts & Templates

Shared script library for your chatters. Create, test and assign templates in just a few clicks.

Scheduling & Tasks

Plan shifts, manage deadlines and priorities for your entire team from a single place.

Content Requests

Centralize requests for your models: photos, videos, customs — with built-in status tracking.

Advanced Analytics

Detailed charts, comparisons, exportable reports — make data-driven decisions.

Finances & Revenue

Revenue per model, commissions, expenses, projections. Your accounting simplified.

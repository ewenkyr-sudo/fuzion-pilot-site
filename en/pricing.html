FAQ

Pricing Questions

Can I start for free?

Yes! The Starter plan is 100% free and lets you test the platform with 1 model and 3 team members. No credit card required.

Can I change plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. The difference is prorated on your next invoice.

Is there a minimum commitment?

No, there is no commitment. You can cancel at any time. If you choose annual billing, you get 20% off and can be refunded within 30 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and SEPA bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Is the Enterprise plan customizable?

Yes, the Enterprise plan is fully customizable. Contact our team for a quote tailored to your specific needs.

Our team is available to help you choose the perfect plan for your agency.
